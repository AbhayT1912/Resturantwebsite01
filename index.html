Our Story

We've beem making healthy food from last 53 years

It all started in 1970 with a dream to start with a Dairy and restaurant by the revered Late Shri Shyam Sundar Harwani. His vision was to highlight Bhopal on the Food map of Madhya Pradesh and excite Bhopaliâ€™s heartbeat with super amazing food, sweets and savoury delights. The dream in a very short while went on to become the most treasured and loved sweet shop and restaurant the town ever had. Manohar Dairy and Restaurant, known for its excellence in quality came up with its first outlet on Hamidia road with the intention of selling dairy products on 15th April 1970 as we were already dealing in supply of milk and milk products in M.P and gradually we moved towards Restaurant and Sweet Business.
